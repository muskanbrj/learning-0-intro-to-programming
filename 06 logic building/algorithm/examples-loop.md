<tr>
<td>Calculate factorial of a number</td>
<td>

1. Let `n` be a number
1. Read `n`
1. Let `f` be `1` and `i` be `1`
1. While `i <= n` loop
1. `f = f * i`
1. `i = i + 1`
1. End while
1. Display `f`
1. Stop

</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Print fibonacci series upto N terms</td>
<td>

1. Let `N` be a number
1. Read `N`
1. Let `a` be `0`, `b` be `1` and `i` be `3`
1. if `N >= 1` then
1. print 0
1. end if
1. if `N >= 2` then
1. print 1
1. end if
1. While `i <= N` loop
1. `c = a + b`
1. print `c`
1. `i = i + 1`
1. End while
1. Display `f`
1. Stop

</td>
</tr>
